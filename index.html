Save Griggs Park

Keep our park open during renovation

What's Happening?

The Town of Brookline is planning to renovate Griggs Park. While we support improving our beloved community space, the current plan would close the entire park for an extended period (around a year, at least).

Our Request

We're asking the Town to select a contractor who will renovate one section at a time, keeping the rest of the park open for families, children, and all community members who depend on this green space.
